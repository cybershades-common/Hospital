What is Spinal Manipulation?

Spinal manipulation is a therapeutic procedure in which trained professionals apply controlled force to specific joints of the spine. This technique can relieve pressure on joints, reduce inflammation, and improve nerve function.

It is especially beneficial for:

  • Lower back pain
  • Neck stiffness
  • Sciatica
  • Spinal misalignments
  • Tension headaches
  • Postural imbalance

Benefits of Spinal Manipulation

  • Immediate pain relief in many cases
  • Increased range of motion
  • Improved posture and spinal alignment
  • Reduced reliance on painkillers
  • Supports faster recovery in rehabilitation
